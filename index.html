Desinging levels:
  • Platforms (Green Cursor)
    1. Press 1
    2. Using the green cursor, click and drag to make platforms of any size
    3. Right click to delete a platform

  • Ladders (Red Cursor)
    1. Press 2
    2. Using the red cursor, click and drag to make ladders of any length
    3. Right click to delete a ladder

  • Doors (Blue Cursor)
    1. Press 3
    2. Using the blue cursor, click to create a door to a new room
    3. After placing a door, enter the name of the door's destination
    4. If no room has that name, a new room will be created
    5. Right click to delete a door
